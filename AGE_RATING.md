## App Functionality

LightBar Control is a simple utility that:

1. **Discovers** Yeelight devices on the local network
2. **Controls** light brightness and color temperature
3. **Manages** ambient light RGB colors
4. **Automates** lighting based on schedules

All functionality is local to the user's network and device. No internet connection is required for core features.

## Parental Guidance

This app is safe for children to use under parental supervision. It controls physical lighting devices in the home.

**Recommendation:** Parents should ensure that:
- The child has permission to control home lighting
- The lighting device is safely installed
