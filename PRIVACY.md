# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 5, 2026**

## Overview

LightBar Control ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

**TL;DR: We don't collect any data. Everything stays on your device and local network.**

---

## Information We Collect

- ‚ùå Device identifiers
- ‚ùå Usage analytics or statistics
- ‚ùå Crash reports
- ‚ùå Location data
- ‚ùå Any form of tracking data

### Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Device Settings**: Your connected Yeelight device information (IP address, device name)
- **App Preferences**: Your preferred settings (auto-connect, automation preferences)
- **Custom Modes**: Any custom lighting modes you create
- **Schedules**: Your lighting schedules

This data is stored using macOS's standard `UserDefaults` and never leaves your device.

---

## Network Communication

### Local Network Only

LightBar Control communicates **exclusively on your local network**:

- **Device Discovery**: Uses SSDP multicast (239.255.255.250:1982) to find Yeelight devices on your local network
- **Device Control**: Connects directly to your Yeelight device via TCP on port 55443

### No Internet Communication

The app does **not**:

- ‚ùå Connect to any external servers
- ‚ùå Send data over the internet
- ‚ùå Require an internet connection to function
- ‚ùå Communicate with Yeelight's cloud services

---

## Third-Party Services

LightBar Control does **not** integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

---

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally and all communication occurs on your local network:

- Your data never travels over the internet
- No external parties have access to your information
- Your privacy is protected by your local network security
